About Us

What is MAMA's MARKET?

Mama’s MARKET is an online platform that brings all market women together all over Africa, allowing the user/buyer locate the nearest shop closest to him. It’s also a personal shopping aid for everyone. We help you shop while you have fun.

The app not only showcases a range of local products, from foodstuff to general commodities needed for survival, but also allows you connect/contact a personal shopping agent.

Why MAMA's MARKET?

A mama’s shop is a Convenience/Provision store which is traditionally located under a high-rise apartment block, or on the street of an African community.

We seek to emulate the fundamental values of such shops - accessibility and expediency along with our goal of saving time and safety, but online.

We aim to provide affordable, safe and hassle-free online shopping experience for everyone in the family through Mama’s MARKET.

With the ever changing needs of people and their continued need for comfort and speed, Mama’s MARKET is aimed at giving everyone the needed comfort and speed to meet their daily food needs and market routines.
